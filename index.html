Biography

Elon Musk, was born on June 28, 1971 in Pretoria, South Africa. He is a entrepreneur, who has left incredible mark on the world by his innovative creations. He owns some of the biggest companies in the world, such as PayPal, SpaceX, Tesla, Twitter. He has become a multi-billionaire in such a short time, and beacause of it he is being true insipiration for upcoming entrepreneur.
